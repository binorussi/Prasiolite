About Us

Prasiolite Trading PLC, established in 2024, is a fresh and energetic company grounded in substantial resources, including a team of experienced professionals, essential equipment, tools, and dependable financial support. The company is poised to undertake a variety of projects and activities, which include:

  • Building, water, and road construction projects, along with related works.
  • Importation of electrical equipment and appliances.
  • Exportation of fruits, vegetables, and oilseeds.
  • Export trade in minerals and mineral products.
  • Export of coffee and tea.
  • Wholesale trade in stationeries, paper, and paper products.
  • Wholesale trade in cosmetics, perfumery, and sanitary articles.
  • Landscape works.

Prasiolite Trading is prepared to engage in these listed activities without geographical limitations, targeting a market that includes private companies, government offices, organizations, agencies, and non-governmental entities. The company is committed to quality delivery within a reasonable timeframe.

Mission

Prasiolite Trading aims to foster professional service delivery characterized by openness and transparency, guided by ethical directives to achieve high performance standards.

Vision

The company's vision is to become a reputable and reliable entity, known for its result-oriented professional services and trade.

Our Services

Construction Services

Building Construction: Our expertise in building construction ranges from residential homes to commercial complexes. We use state-of-the-art technology and high-quality materials to ensure that every project is completed to the highest standards.

Water Construction: We specialize in constructing water infrastructure, including dams, water treatment plants, and irrigation systems. Our projects aim to provide sustainable water solutions that support communities and industries.

Road Construction: Our road construction services include highways, urban roads, and rural paths. We focus on creating durable and efficient roadways that enhance connectivity and support economic growth.

Import/Export Services

Electrical Equipment: We import and export a wide range of electrical equipment, including transformers, cables, and generators. Our products meet international standards and are sourced from reputable manufacturers.

Agricultural Products: We trade in agricultural products such as grains, fruits, and vegetables. Our network ensures that we can deliver fresh and high-quality produce to our clients.

Minerals: We facilitate the trade of various minerals, including gold, copper, and iron ore. Our operations are compliant with industry regulations and focused on sustainability.

Coffee and Tea: We offer premium quality coffee and tea, sourced from the best plantations. Our products are known for their rich flavors and are in high demand globally.

Wholesale Trade

Stationery and Paper Products: We supply a variety of stationery and paper products for schools, offices, and businesses. Our inventory includes notebooks, printing paper, and writing instruments.

Cosmetics: We trade in a range of cosmetics, including skincare, haircare, and beauty products. Our offerings are from trusted brands known for their quality and effectiveness.

Sanitary Articles: Our wholesale trade includes sanitary products such as diapers, sanitary napkins, and personal hygiene items. We ensure that all products meet health and safety standards.

Landscape Services

Residential Landscaping: We provide comprehensive landscaping services for residential properties, including garden design, installation, and maintenance. Our team ensures that your outdoor space is beautiful and functional.

Commercial Landscaping: Our commercial landscaping services enhance the aesthetic appeal of business premises, parks, and public spaces. We focus on sustainable practices and innovative designs to create attractive landscapes.

Our Projects

Residential Building Project - Addis Ababa

We completed a state-of-the-art residential complex in Addis Ababa, featuring modern amenities, sustainable materials, and innovative design. The project has provided comfortable and stylish living spaces for over 200 families.

Irrigation System - Oromia Region

Our team successfully implemented an advanced irrigation system in the Oromia region, significantly improving agricultural productivity. This project has helped local farmers increase their yields and ensure food security.

Highway Construction - Awash to Dire Dawa

We constructed a major highway connecting Awash to Dire Dawa, enhancing transportation and trade in the region. The highway is built to international standards and is expected to boost economic activities.

Mineral Export Project - Tigray

We facilitated the export of high-quality gold and copper from Tigray, adhering to strict environmental and safety regulations. This project has contributed to the local economy and supported sustainable mining practices.

Coffee Export - Sidamo to Europe

Our coffee export project involved sourcing premium coffee beans from Sidamo and distributing them to various European markets. The project highlighted our commitment to quality and efficient supply chain management.

Commercial Landscaping - Addis Ababa

We transformed the Addis Ababa Business District with our commercial landscaping services, creating green spaces and enhancing the urban environment. The project has made the district more attractive and environmentally friendly.
